3. Site & Competitive Analysis

Storage Central Site Issues

  • Navigation: Cluttered layout, unclear CTAs, poor content flow
  • Design: Inconsistent branding, outdated imagery, accessibility issues
  • Content: Hidden text, redundant information, low contrast

Competitor Insights

Competitor Strengths Weaknesses
Lenovo Branding, navigation Cluttered layout, inconsistent design
